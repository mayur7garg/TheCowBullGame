How to play

The Cow Bull Game

Your goal is guess a English word using a maximum of 10 attempts.

The word you need to guess to win:

  • is a meaningful dictionary English word.
  • is 4 characters in length.
  • has no repeated characters.

All your guesses are case insensitive.

For every guess you make, you would be given the number of BULLS and COWS you hit. You can use that info to further narrow down your search and make better gueses.

Your guesses needn't be meaningful English words but must be 4 characters long with no repeating characters.

Your first attempt would always be a shot in the dark!

If a letter in your guess is present in the word that you need to guess and is also in the same position, then you would hit a BULL.

For example:

If the word you needed to guess was COWS, and your guess was WORD, you will hit 1 BULL as the letter O is present in both COWS and WORD and is at the same position i.e. is the 2nd character in both.

If a letter in your guess is present in the word that you need to guess and is but is in a different position in both, then you would hit a COW.

For example:

If the word you needed to guess was COWS, and your guess was WORD, you will hit 1 COW as the letter W is present in both COWS and WORD but is in a different position in both (3rd and 1st respectively).
